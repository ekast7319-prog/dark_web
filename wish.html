عيد مبارك
Eid Mubarak
Blessed Eid to You & Yours

May this blessed Eid fill your home with joy, your heart with peace,
and your days with the warmth of Allah's infinite mercy.
May your prayers be answered and your fasts accepted.

تَقَبَّلَ اللّهُ مِنَّا وَمِنكُم
Taqabbalallahu minna wa minkum
May Allah accept (the worship) from us and from you.

"If you are grateful, I will surely increase you [ in favor ]." — Surah Ibrahim 14:7

Eid Duas

Beautiful supplications for this blessed day

تَقَبَّلَ اللّهُ مِنَّا وَمِنكُم
Taqabbalallahu minna wa minkum
May Allah accept (good deeds) from us and from you.
اللَّهُمَّ إِنَّكَ عَفُوٌّ تُحِبُّ الْعَفْوَ فَاعْفُ عَنِّي
Allahumma innaka 'afuwwun tuhibbul 'afwa fa'fu 'anni
O Allah, You are Pardoning and You love pardon, so pardon me.
رَبَّنَا آتِنَا فِي الدُّنْيَا حَسَنَةً وَفِي الآخِرَةِ حَسَنَةً
Rabbana atina fid-dunya hasanatan wa fil-akhirati hasanatan
Our Lord, give us good in this world and good in the Hereafter.
اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَنَا فِيمَا رَزَقْتَنَا
Allahumma barik lana fima razaqtana
O Allah, bless us in what You have provided for us.
